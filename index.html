Penetration Testing Services

  • Free Services
  • Premium Services
    • In-Depth Vulnerability Assessment
    • Web Application Security Implementation

I can perform a short vulnerability assessment on your website/web application and provide you with a brief report on my findings. The report will not include remediation strategies but it will give you an overview of the security of your website. The assessment will take upto a week and the report will be sent via email.

I will carry out a deep vulnerability assessment on your website/web application and facilitate you with an in-depth vulnerability analysis report. I will include remediation strategies in my report and I will work with your security/developer team to implement proper measures to patch vulnerabilities. If I am unable to find any vulnerabilities in your application, I will not charge any fee.

I will add appropriate security barriers to your website/web application by incorporating a Web Application Firewall. The firewall will block SQL Injection, XSS (Cross Site Scripting), CSRF (Cross Site Request Forgery), and DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) Attacks. I will also enable HTTPS on your website to prevent packet-sniffing attacks.
